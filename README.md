I wanted to make a project representing the concept of getting a virus when searching on the internet. So I made it look like a search engine. I wanted to give it the image of having customisation - hence the background. The engine is meant to appear safe, it's used trusthworthingly - under the guise that location and ip is hidden. It will have something similar to a Tor circuit to stimulate to concept of saftey, however in really it is not - the circuit is fake, an illusion.

The overall concept is to represent the idea of a student studying, seaching on the internet for their assinments only to click on a link and get sent a notification that their ip is now exposed.
